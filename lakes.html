Bellandur Lake

Bellandur Lake is the largest lake in Bengaluru and has faced pollution problems due to sewage inflow.

Ulsoor Lake

Ulsoor Lake is a historic lake located near the city centre and is a popular recreational area.

Sankey Tank

Sankey Tank is one of the cleanest lakes in Bengaluru and a major green space for citizens.
